Bad Boyz of BBQ's signature rubs and open-flame grill bring out the unique flavors in every piece of meat we cook. Starting with Choice beef, we cook our meats to the peak of perfection. We apply our signature rubs and marinades to allow the flavors to permeate the meat for at least 24 hours before we grill our chicken and tri tips on our open-flame grill. All meats cooked to Medium unless otherwise requested. Brisket , Ribs and Pulled pork are slow smoked to perfection in our smokers.
